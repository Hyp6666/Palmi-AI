# 掌心AI / Pocket AI 隐私政策

更新日期：2026年3月9日

## 中文版

### 隐私政策

欢迎使用掌心AI。

我们尽量用最直接的方式说明这款 App 如何处理你的信息。

掌心AI是一款以本地运行为主的应用。你在 App 中输入的文字、上传的图片、生成的内容和聊天记录，默认都只保存在你的设备上，不会上传到开发者的服务器，也不会由开发者进行远程查看、分析或存储。

### 1. 我们会处理哪些信息

在你使用掌心AI时，App 可能会处理以下内容：

- 你主动输入的文字内容
- 你主动选择的图片
- App 在本地生成的回复内容
- 保存在本地设备中的聊天记录和相关设置

这些内容仅用于实现 App 的核心功能，例如本地问答、图片相关对话和历史记录保存。

### 2. 这些信息在哪里处理

掌心AI的主要处理过程发生在你的设备本地。

这意味着：

- 你的文字内容不会上传到开发者服务器
- 你的图片不会上传到开发者服务器
- 你的聊天记录不会上传到开发者服务器
- 开发者不会通过后台系统收集你的对话内容

### 3. 关于图片

如果你在 App 中选择图片，这些图片仅用于你当前在设备上的使用过程。

图片相关处理为本地处理，不会因为使用掌心AI而自动上传到互联网，也不会上传到开发者服务器。图片是否继续保存在你的设备上，取决于你的本地使用和系统存储状态。

### 4. 本地存储

为了让你能够继续查看历史内容和使用已有设置，掌心AI会在你的设备本地保存部分数据，例如聊天记录、界面设置和使用偏好。

这些数据由你自己的设备控制。你可以通过删除聊天记录、清除 App 数据或卸载 App 的方式移除这些本地内容。

### 5. 我们不做的事情

掌心AI当前不以开发者服务器为基础提供服务，因此我们不会主动进行以下行为：

- 不会要求你注册开发者账号体系
- 不会将你的聊天内容上传到开发者服务器
- 不会将你上传的图片上传到开发者服务器
- 不会在开发者服务器中建立你的个人资料档案

### 6. 第三方与系统服务

掌心AI本身不把你的聊天文字和图片发送到开发者服务器。

但如果你通过 Apple 提供的系统环境下载 App、获取系统服务或使用操作系统本身的功能，相关数据可能仍会按照 Apple 的规则处理。这部分内容受 Apple 自身的隐私政策约束，不由掌心AI单独控制。

### 7. 儿童隐私

掌心AI不会故意向儿童收集个人信息。由于本 App 主要在本地运行，开发者通常也不会直接接触到你在 App 中输入或选择的内容。

### 8. 隐私政策变更

如果未来掌心AI的功能发生变化，导致信息处理方式出现实质调整，我们会同步更新本隐私政策，并在适当位置说明更新后的内容。

### 9. 联系方式

如果你对本隐私政策有疑问，可以通过 App Store 页面中的开发者联系方式与我们联系。

## English Version

### Privacy Policy

Welcome to Pocket AI.

We want to explain in the clearest possible way how this app handles your information.

Pocket AI is an app designed to run primarily on your device. The text you enter, the images you choose, the content generated by the app, and your chat history are stored on your device by default. They are not uploaded to the developer's servers, and the developer does not remotely view, analyze, or store them.

### 1. What information may be processed

When you use Pocket AI, the app may process the following content:

- Text that you enter voluntarily
- Images that you choose voluntarily
- Responses generated locally by the app
- Chat history and related settings stored on your device

This content is used only to provide the core functions of the app, such as on-device question answering, image-related conversations, and local history storage.

### 2. Where this information is processed

Pocket AI mainly processes information locally on your device.

This means:

- Your text is not uploaded to the developer's servers
- Your images are not uploaded to the developer's servers
- Your chat history is not uploaded to the developer's servers
- The developer does not collect your conversation content through a backend system

### 3. About images

If you choose images in the app, those images are used only for your current experience on your device.

Image-related processing is performed locally. Using Pocket AI does not automatically upload your images to the internet or to the developer's servers. Whether those images remain stored on your device depends on your local usage and system storage status.

### 4. Local storage

To let you continue viewing past content and using your existing settings, Pocket AI stores some data locally on your device, such as chat history, interface settings, and usage preferences.

This data is controlled by your own device. You can remove local content by deleting chat history, clearing app data, or uninstalling the app.

### 5. What we do not do

Pocket AI does not currently rely on developer-operated servers to provide its service, so we do not actively do the following:

- We do not require you to register for a developer account system
- We do not upload your chat content to the developer's servers
- We do not upload your images to the developer's servers
- We do not build a personal profile about you on the developer's servers

### 6. Third parties and system services

Pocket AI itself does not send your chat text or images to the developer's servers.

However, if you download the app through Apple, use Apple-provided system services, or use operating system features, related data may still be handled according to Apple's own rules. That part is governed by Apple's privacy policy and is not separately controlled by Pocket AI.

### 7. Children's privacy

Pocket AI does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app mainly runs locally, the developer usually does not directly access the content you enter or choose in the app.

### 8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

If Pocket AI changes in the future and the way information is handled changes in a material way, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly and explain the updated content where appropriate.

### 9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the developer contact information shown on the App Store page.
